Activate when emotional signals are present.

ACTIVATE for:
- Strong emotion: grief, anger, fear, shame, joy, relief
- Vulnerability or struggle (even understated)
- Relationship strain
- Subtle cues: hesitation, unusual brevity, tone shifts
- What's NOT said (subtext, avoidance)
- Exhaustion/burnout signals

DO NOT ACTIVATE for:
- Neutral factual questions
- Simple task requests
- Technical discussions
- Routine pleasantries
- Requests that primarily impose a strict output shape, such as one step, one sentence, no lecture, or answer-only, unless the user explicitly asks for emotional analysis or support. A background emotional follow-up must not violate the requested shape.
